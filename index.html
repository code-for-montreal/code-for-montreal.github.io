About us

We are Montrealers who are putting technology to work for the benefit of our community. In an increasingly digital world, small organizations struggle to finance expensive development work, and connecting them with skilled developers is a great way for the tech community to give back. We are developers, designers, researchers, and curious citizens who want to make Montreal more just, equitable, transparent, and delightful to live in through data, design, and technology. Come join us or submit an idea!
